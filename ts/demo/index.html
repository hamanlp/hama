hama engine playground

G2P, ASR, and P2G running entirely in your browser on the hand-written WASM engine (~39 KB, no onnxruntime). Each model's weights load on first use.

—
Idle — pick a clip; the ASR model (~5 MB) loads on first run.

Tip: clearest results on 16 kHz English speech; any sample rate is resampled automatically. The word boundaries (|) sent to P2G are the ASR model's own predicted <wb> tokens — not VAD.

Idle — click to load the P2G model (~29 MB).

Heads up: P2G is a small (~7.3M-param) ~56% WER research model, so its text is deliberately approximate (e.g. quilter → kilter). The demo is about engine speed and exact-parity behavior, not transcription accuracy.
